## Blanket orders is a long-term purchase agreements with our vendors to receive products overtime instead of one big shipping

Enable `Purchase Agreements` in purchase settings

Go to Purchase -> Order -> Purchase Agreements

Create new record and select `Blanket Order` in Agreement Type

Set agreement validity from start of the year to end of the year or set it to blank

Click `New Qu0otation` button to create RFQ

On the balnket order form, check the `Ordered` column.
