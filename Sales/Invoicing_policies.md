<u>Ordered Quantities</u> - When they plate the order

<u>Delivered Quantities</u> - When order is delivered, invoice and goods will be delivered at the same time

Delivered Quantities is good for businesses that frequently ship large quantities of goods or typically deals with discrepancies between ordered and delivered quantities

<u>Milestone</u> - Reaching milestone will customer pay

<u>Timesheets</u> - based on workhours

set default in Sales Settings

set automatic invoice in settings to generate invoice automatically when online payment is confirmed
