## Pipeline Management for a B2B Equipment Supplier

Open website, modify action of `Contact Us` to `Create Opportunity`

Go to CRM -> Leads and find there the created opportunity

set the priority

click `Convert to opportunity`

Update expected revenue field

Click `New quotation` -> Send by email

set the state of opportunity to `Proposition`

Schedule an activity by clicking `Activity` in the chatter

Click `Mark Done`, add feedback, then done

Update revenue and quotation if needed

update state to won or lost
