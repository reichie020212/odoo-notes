## Lots and Serial Numbers

Lots - a group of identical items received, stored, or shipped together. Used for tracking all items in the group

Serial Number - Unique identifying number

A __Lot__ is useful for products you make or receive in large quantities

Serial numbers are good for products that require after-sales services

Enable `lots and serial numbers` in config settings

Go to products form then set the track inventory to `By lots` or `By Unique Serial Number`

Product type should be a `goods`

In the PO stock picking, click the hamburger icon of a line order

Generate or manually add or import lot or serial numbers

Validate stock pickings

`Traceability` smart button will show. Click it to see Traceability Report

In sales, validate SO then click `Delivery` smart button then validate it.

Go to Traceability by clicking `Traceability` smart button

To check the high level view of lots and Serial Number, go to Inventory -> Products -> Lots/Serial Number

Open one record to see all smart buttons as well as other details of the lot or S/N
