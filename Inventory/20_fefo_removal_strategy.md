## FEFO Removal Strategy

Enablle `Lots and S/N` and `Expiration Dates`

Product must be track by lots or S/N. Also, check the `Expiration Date` field, date fields related to expiration will show.

Go to products -> lots/serial numbers menu

check the columsn `removal date` and `expiration date`

date fields related to expiration can be set in lots or S/N records

Set the removal strategy to either the `location` or `product category`

in stock picking, check in hamburger icon if the removal strategy takes place
