## Print Shipping Label

Enable any shipping connectors and their shipping methods

go to the shipping method and set the configurations and other details

create a quotation then click `add shipping` button. This button will show a pop-up window

Select prefereed Shipping Method, then click `Get Rate` button, then add the shipping.

Confirm the quotation and validate the stock picking

after validating, label shipping PDF will be added in the chatter.
