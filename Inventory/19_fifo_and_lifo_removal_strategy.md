## FIFO and LIFO Removal Strategy

Enable `Lots and Serial Numbers` in config settings

enable also `storage locations` and `multi-step routes`

go to locations form to set the removal strategy

you can also set a `force removal strategy` to a product category by going to configuration -> categories

go to stock pickings then click hamburger icon to see if the removal strategy takes place

product must be track by lots or S/N
