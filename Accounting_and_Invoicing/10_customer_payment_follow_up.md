## Customer Payment follow-up

Go to Configuration -> follow-up levels to configure

Go to Customer -> Invoices then filter to `Overdue` to see all Overdue invoices

Go to Customers -> Customers -> Accounting Tab to see `Invoice follow-ups` configs

Go to Customer list view, filter by either `Overdue invoices` or `Requires follow-up`

Select customers -> click Action then `Process foloow-ups`

Go to Reporting -> Partner Ledger then select `Follow-up Report` to see Reports

to check a customers's total overdue amount, go to customer form then click the `Customer Statement` or `Due` smart button
