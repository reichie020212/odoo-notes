## Getting Started

Database will look different depending on company's fiscal localization since different country have different requirements

Note: Once you make your first journal entry, you wont be able to change the fiscal localization

Journal item is either a debit or a credit which is an amount that is added to or subtracted from an account

Accounting -> Journal Items

can be group by Journal Entry

Configurations -> Journals

Journals is simply a collection of Journal Entry and they are useful for keeping eerything organized

Reporting -> General Ledger

General Ledger is a view of all accounts with each accounts balance of debits and credits
