## Manufacturing Backorders

Create an MO with atleast 2 quantity

Go to Shop Floor App -> Work Center -> Click Register Production -> Update the quantity produced to less than the quantity set then Validate -> click Mark as Done

<img width="749" height="372" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6b459830-50cf-4ada-985a-c73810a9f112" />

Go to the next work center and you cna see that the MO name has `001` now, this confirms that the MO has been splitted automatically

Refresh the browser and go back to the `All MO` dashboard, you can see here the splitted MOs
