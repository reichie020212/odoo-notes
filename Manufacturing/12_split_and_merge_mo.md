## Split and Merge Manufacturing Orders

Split - Split single MO into multiple MOs for smaller quantities

Merge - allows to merge multiple MOs of the same product into a single MO

to split MO, create MO with many quantities, confirm it, click the gear icon, click `Split` action.

By clicking `Split` action, a pop-up window will show, enter the number of split. Table is automatically set based on number of split but they can be updated

<img width="1567" height="697" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/293b64ad-bdce-4a99-9459-4d9d0ed985cd" />

click `Split` button to split the MO. You will be automatically redirected to the first MO

to see all the splitted MO, click the `Backorder` smart button.

to merge MOs, go to Manufacturing Orders list view, select atleast two MOs with the same product, click `Actions`, then click `Merge`.

You will be automatically redirected to the merged MO

Go back to the list view and you will see in the `Source` column the name of MO that merged to create this single MO
